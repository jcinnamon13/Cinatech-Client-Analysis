# CinaTech: AI-Powered Strategic Analysis for Marketing Agencies

## What is CinaTech?
CinaTech is an AI-powered strategic analysis tool built specifically for marketing agencies. It transforms standard client onboarding documents into comprehensive, actionable strategic intelligence in minutes. 

## The Problem
When a marketing agency takes on a new client, the initial discovery and strategy phase requires hours of manual research. Account managers must analyse the client's industry, perform competitive gap analysis, check for regulatory compliance issues, and draft an initial strategy plan. This manual workflow is time-consuming, prone to error, and delays the launch of revenue-generating campaigns.

## The Solution
CinaTech automates this entire strategic teardown. By simply uploading a new client's onboarding form, CinaTech's AI engine instantly generates a full, bespoke strategic report. It replaces manual heavy-lifting with an instant, expert-level strategic brief.

## What Every Report Includes
1. **Competitive Gap Analysis:** Identifies exactly where the client's competitors are winning and highlights strategic gaps the agency can exploit.
2. **Legal & Compliance Risk Flags:** Automatically checks sector-specific regulations, identifying legal risks (e.g., in healthcare, finance, or aesthetics) before the first paid ad or outbound campaign goes live.
3. **Priority Action Plan:** A ranked, prioritised list of recommendations written specifically for that client, detailing the highest-leverage starting points.
4. **Sector-Specific Strategic Insight:** Contextual intelligence drawn from the client's exact industry rather than generic marketing advice.
